About my current project

Scaling Up Industries is a Sci-fi FPS that will blow your socks off! You are a undercover operative on the mission to destroy the alien science facility known as Scaling Up Industries and save humanity. Do you have what it takes to defeat what horrors you might find inside?

The intense gameplay mixed with the original made, kick-ass soundtrack is sure to keep you on the edge of your seat from beginning to end!

Made for the GameOff 2023 game jam held by GitHub.
